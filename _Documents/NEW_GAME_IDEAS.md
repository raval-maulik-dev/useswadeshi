# 🎮 New Game Ideas

Below is the curated list of new game concepts included in `database/seeders/GameSeeder.php`, with concise descriptions suitable for product planning and development.

- **Supply Chain Explorer**
  - Description: Trace the journey of a product from raw material to consumer and identify local vs. foreign dependencies.

- **Local Treasure Hunt (Digital)**
  - Description: A puzzle-style game where players unlock clues about famous Indian products and brands.

- **Sustainable Switch**
  - Description: Players choose between two product options and get points for selecting eco-friendly local alternatives.

- **Make in India Memory Match**
  - Description: A memory card-flip game matching Indian brands to their products.

- **Local Startups Quiz**
  - Description: Identify upcoming Indian startups and their innovations.

- **Price Comparison Challenge**
  - Description: Compare the cost of foreign vs. local products and guess which is more affordable.

- **Swadeshi Crossword**
  - Description: A crossword puzzle featuring Indian brands, festivals, and entrepreneurs.

- **Impact Meter**
  - Description: Enter your monthly purchases and see the impact of choosing local (savings + environment).

- **Guess the Origin**
  - Description: Identify the country of origin for famous brands (bonus points for choosing the Indian alternative).

- **Local Legends Trivia**
  - Description: Fun quiz about historic Indian brands, businesses, and their founders.
